## What is OCA?

The **Office of Cadet Affairs (OCA)** exists to help cadets grow in:

- **Character**  
- **Leadership**  
- **Academics**  
- **Physical Fitness**  
- **Lifeskills (CLAP-L)**  

We work closely with Formation Officers, Fleet Officers, and student leaders to make sure cadets are:

- Guided  
- Supported  
- Accountable  
- Equipped to lead, not just comply.
