Welcome to the OCA – Leading Lights Portal

This site serves as the central hub for the Office of Cadet Affairs (OCA): guidelines, processes, leadership systems, and resources that support the holistic formation of MMMA cadets.

Purpose of this site:
To make OCA systems clear, consistent, and accessible – so that Fleet Officers, Formation Officers, and cadet leaders know exactly how to execute with discipline, excellence, and service.

OCA Framework

  • CLAP-L Domains: Character, Leadership, Academics, Physical Fitness, Lifeskills
  • Code for Life & core values: Discipline, Excellence, Service
  • Transformational, developmental approach (not fear-based)

For Fleet & Team Leaders

  • Turnover playbooks and expectations
  • Standard routines, formations, and systems
  • Sample scripts, memos, and templates

For Formation Officers

  • Coaching guidelines and CLDP structure
  • Leadership grading and 360 evaluation overview
  • Suggested interventions and developmental pathways

For Cadet Leaders

  • How to succeed in your leadership post
  • Merit/Demerit basics & accountability systems
  • Best practices from previous fleets
